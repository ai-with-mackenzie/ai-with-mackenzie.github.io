Why live training?

I strongly prefer live trainings over on-demand courses. On-demand courses teach you what someone else thought you needed to know. Live sessions are built around you: your tools, your workflow, your questions. You learn faster, retain more, and leave every session with something you can use immediately.

In all honesty, the reason I do live training is simpler than that. I genuinely love the personal component of live trainings. I love the moment when something clicks for someone, and I love being able to facilitate that. I love being able to read the room, slow down when something isn't landing, and go deeper when someone is ready to push further. That back-and-forth is what makes learning stick!

I want every session to feel personal, because I genuinely care that you walk away feeling like it was worth your time and money. I've seen what happens when someone finally gets it, and I want that for every single person I work with. That's something a pre-recorded course just can't give you.
